Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onion and minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add ground beef or sausage to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, eggs, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  6. Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Layer cooked lasagna noodles, meat mixture, ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of noodles topped with marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  9. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top.
  10. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  12. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
